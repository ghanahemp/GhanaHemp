Important — We Cannot Help With Licences

GhanaHemp.com is a news publication. We cannot process, assist with, or advise on NACOC cannabis licence applications. For licensing, contact NACOC directly: portal.ncc.gov.gh · Toll-Free: 0800 307 307 · Email: cannabis@ncc.gov.gh. Beware of anyone claiming they can get you a licence for a fee — NACOC has officially warned no intermediaries are authorised.
