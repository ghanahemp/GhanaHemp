For Cannabis Licence Applications, Contact NACOC Directly

GhanaHemp.com is a news publication. We cannot process licence applications, advise on applications, or represent applicants. All licensing matters must go directly to NACOC. Beware of anyone claiming to represent or expedite NACOC applications — this is fraudulent.
