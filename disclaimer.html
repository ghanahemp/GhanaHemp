Recreational Cannabis Is Illegal in Ghana

The recreational use, possession, purchase, sale, cultivation, and distribution of cannabis remains a criminal offence under Ghanaian law, including the Narcotics Control Commission Act 1019 (2020) and subsequent legislation. GhanaHemp.com covers only the legal, licensed industrial hemp and medicinal cannabis sector. Nothing on this site should be construed as encouraging, facilitating, or condoning illegal activity.

Official Licensing Authority

The Narcotics Control Commission (NACOC) is Ghana's sole regulatory authority for cannabis licensing. GhanaHemp.com is not affiliated with NACOC, the Ministry of the Interior, or any government body. We do not facilitate, expedite, process, or advise on licence applications. Apply directly and only through portal.ncc.gov.gh. NACOC toll-free: 0800 307 307. Beware of any individual or organisation claiming to facilitate NACOC licences — NACOC has formally warned this is fraudulent.
